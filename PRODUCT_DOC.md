# Beauticate OS: The Ultimate Agency Operating System

## Executive Summary
**Beauticate OS** is a premium, all-in-one management platform specifically engineered for modern media and content agencies. It bridges the gap between sales, onboarding, and high-volume production, providing a single source of truth for founders, editors, and clients.

Unlike generic project management tools, Beauticate OS is built with a **production-first** philosophy, focusing on the unique bottlenecks of content creation—such as editor capacity, revision tracking, and multi-platform publishing workflows.

## 1. Product Scope & Core Modules

### Sales CRM (Leads)
*   **Pipeline Management**: Track leads from initial contact to "Closed Won" status.
*   **Revenue Forecasting**: Estimate potential revenue and track closing dates.
*   **Centralized Notes**: Keep all prospect communications and discovery notes in one place.

### Standard Operating Procedure (Onboarding)
*   **Automated Checklist**: Ensure every client experience is consistent. Track contract signatures, invoice payments, and strategy calls.
*   **Account Assignment**: Seamlessly assign Editors and Social Managers during the transition from Lead to Client.
*   **Rapid Handover**: Instant access to client packages and specific requirements for the production team.

### Production Pipeline (Videos)
*   **Unified Dashboard**: Real-time view of every video in production.
*   **Status Tracking**: Granular tracking from 'Not Started' to 'Editing', 'Delivered', 'Revision', and 'Approved'.
*   **Platform Specifics**: Track target platforms (TikTok, Reels, etc.) and posting status (Pending, Scheduled, Posted).
*   **Revision Control**: Monitor how many revisions are used per project to protect agency margins.

### Resource Management (Editors)
*   **Capacity Tracking**: Monitor "Weekly Video Caps" for every editor.
*   **Workload Balancing**: Real-time visibility into who is over-leveraged and who has space for new projects.
*   **Role-Based Access**: Specialized views for Superadmins, Admins, Editors, and Social Managers.

### Ad Campaign Management
*   **Performance Overview**: Monitor active, paused, and draft campaigns.
*   **Budgeting**: Track budget vs. actual spend across platforms like Meta.
*   **Creative Assets**: Direct links to campaign creatives and optimization schedules.

### Knowledge Base & SOPs
*   **Internal Wiki**: Store agency-specific Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
*   **Asset Library**: Centralized storage for attachments, images, and documents.

## 2. Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

### Phase 1: Lead to Client Conversion
1.  **Capture**: Sales team enters a new lead into the **Leads** module.
2.  **Proposal**: Move lead through stages (Call -> Proposal -> Follow Up).
3.  **Close**: When marked "Closed Won," a record is automatically prepared for onboarding.

### Phase 2: Onboarding Flow
1.  **Kickoff**: Admin initializes the **Onboarding** checklist.
2.  **Assignment**: Select the Lead Editor and Social Media Manager based on current capacity.
3.  **Verification**: Tick off technical steps (Strategy Call, Invoice Paid) before graduating to "Active" status.

### Phase 3: Production Cycle
1.  **Drafting**: New video records are created for the client's monthly quota.
2.  **Editing**: Editor moves video to "Editing" once footage is received.
3.  **Approval**: Client/Quality Control moves video to "Revision" or "Approved."
4.  **Distribution**: Social Media Manager handles "Scheduling" and "Posting."

### Phase 4: Performance Review
1.  **Dashboard Analysis**: Agency owners review the **Analytics Dashboard** for revenue growth and editor output.
2.  **Optimization**: Adjust editor caps or client packages based on production data.

## 3. Technical Architecture
Beauticate OS is built on a modern, high-performance tech stack designed for speed and reliability:

*   **Frontend**: Next.js 16+ (React 19) for a snappy, app-like experience.
*   **Backend/Database**: Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row-Level Security (RLS) for enterprise-grade data isolation.
*   **Styling**: Tailwind CSS for a clean, minimal "Beauticate" aesthetic.
*   **State Management**: Real-time synchronization for collaborative production tracking.
*   **Monitoring**: Integrated with Sentry for proactive error tracking.
